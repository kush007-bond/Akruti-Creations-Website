# Future Implementations & Enhancements

This document outlines potential features, animations, and pages to further elevate the Akruti Creations brand experience.

## ✨ Advanced Animations
- **Custom Cursor Interaction**: A custom SVG cursor that morphs or changes color based on the surface it hovers over (e.g., expanding when hovering over projects).
- **Smooth Scroll (Lenis/GSAP)**: Implementation of a smooth-scrolling library to provide a more luxurious, fluid feel to the vertical movement.
- **GLSL Shaders for Backgrounds**: Subtly moving liquid or grain shaders to add organic texture to section backgrounds.
- **Image Distortions on Hover**: Using WebGL to create "water ripple" or "lens" effects when hovering over project thumbnails.
- **Split-Text Header Animations**: Individual character reveals for section titles to create a high-end editorial entry effect.

## 📄 New Page Concepts
- **The Laboratory (Experimental Work)**: A page dedicated to R&D, fiber experiments, and unfinished sculptural concepts that show the "behind-the-scenes" craft.
- **Client Portal**: A secure area for clients to view project progress, blueprints, and high-resolution print proofs.
- **Press & Recognition**: A visual timeline of awards, news features, and city-scale contributions (like historical Lalbagh highlights).
- **Deep-Dive Case Studies**: Dynamic, long-form project pages with video loops, technical drawings, and material samples.

## 🛠 Functional Enhancements
- **Dark/Light Mode Toggle**: A sophisticated theme switcher that transitions between the Charcoal/Mustard and Off-white/Mustard palettes.
- **Multi-language Support**: Specialized localizations for regional Bangalore projects and international brand partners.
- **Interactive Map**: A 3D or stylized SVG map on the Contact page showing the location of major public installations across the city.
